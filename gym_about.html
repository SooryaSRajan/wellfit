Learn More About our Studio Classes

Enjoy all the fitness classes your love led by our amazing instructors who challenge you to push harder to meet all your fitness goals.

STRENGTH CLASSES

Get stronger and fitter with our exclusive Strength Classes that challenges your entire body. Do more sets, reps and crunches with our highly experienced trainers to achieve to total-body results.

CARDIO CLASSES

Attend our Cardio Classes to stay fit and healthy. These highly energising workouts are designed to melt all your calories out while boosting your fitness and morale to attain your goals. Burn your way to a better day.

YOGA CLASSES

Awaken your body and mind with our Yoga Classes to breath away all your stress from a rough days work. With gentle postures and breathing excersices improve your flexibility and leve class feeling calm and serene.

DANCE CLASSES

Burn out all your calories with our music driven Dance Classes. With our Zumba and Hip Hop classes enjoy high energy workouts enjoy the best best hour of your day. Dance the night (or day) away and get fit with every step and beat.

FREESTYLE CLASSES

Our Freestyle Group Training (FGT) is a combination of three-dimensional training movements that will challenge your muscles to move you in the way nature intended. Each of the challenging FGT classes are designed to help you reach your goals, whilst improving your everyday movement ability.
